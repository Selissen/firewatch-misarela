How the signal is built

Two reads are blended by how far off your trip is. Far out, the forecast for your dates leads and live fires are treated as "how volatile is this corridor." As check-in nears, live proximity and smoke take over — a fire on the doorstep the day before matters more than any forecast.

Cautionactive fire within ~25 km, or Very-high fire weather, or upwind fire
High concernsignificant active fire within ~10 km / upwind, or Extreme fire weather

Fire weather (0–100, indicative) rises with heat, low humidity and wind, falls with rain, and is scaled by drought memory — the last 30 days of rainfall at this point. Threat ranking weights each fire by distance, size (crew & aircraft committed), upwind position and lifecycle status, so a large fire winding down counts less than a small one taking hold nearby.

The realistic risk for a guest

The hotel burning is unlikely; the practical disruptions are smoke, a precautionary evacuation, or the access road (CM1397) being cut — any of which can happen without the fire reaching you. Treat a close upwind fire as a reason to call the hotel about access, not just to read the map.
